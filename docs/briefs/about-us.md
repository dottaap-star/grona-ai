# About Us
> Route: `/about-us` | Phase: 6 | Action: Revise
> Design ref: [page-design.md](../../.claude/rules/page-design.md) | Strategy ref: [website-plan.md](../website-plan.md)

## Purpose
Humanize the company. Answer "who's behind this?" Especially important for a young company. Visitors want real people behind a product that handles their live website. Lead with mission and traction, not just bios.

## Audience & Funnel Stage
- **Who**: Visitors vetting the company's legitimacy and longevity
- **How they arrive**: Footer, nav, organic search, late-stage evaluation
- **Mindset**: "Is this a real company? Will they be around next year?"
- **Funnel**: Trust-building (any stage)

## Primary Conversion
- **Path**: Soft link
- **Mechanism**: CTA at bottom linking to features or homepage
- **Destination**: `/features` or homepage
- **Secondary**: None. This page's job is trust.

---

## Sections

### S1: Hero
**Job**: Lead with mission, not history. Per page-design.md: "Lead with mission, not history."
**Spec**:
- H1: mission-focused
- Subheadline explaining what Grona exists to change
**Key lines**:
- H1: "Making CRO accessible to every team"
- Sub: "We believe every business should be able to optimize their website without hiring an agency or waiting on developers."

---

### S2: The Problem We're Solving
**Job**: Frame the mission as a response to a real, specific problem.
**Spec**:
- 2-3 paragraphs on:
  - CRO has always been expensive and technical
  - Tools like VWO cost $199+/mo and need developers
  - Agencies charge $3K-$10K/mo
  - Small teams are locked out of the strategies that drive growth
- Connect to Grona's approach: AI + chat interface = accessible CRO
**Design**: Short paragraphs. 600-750px reading width.

---

### S3: Team
**Job**: Put real faces and backgrounds behind the product.
**Spec**:
- Founder/team photos (real, not stock)
- Name, role, brief background
- Focus on relevant experience (CRO, AI, marketing tech)
**Design**: Per page-design.md about page rules: "Real team photos. Company metrics as trust signals."

---

### S4: Traction / Metrics
**Job**: Signal momentum. "This isn't a weekend project."
**Spec**:
- 2-4 company metrics:
  - Websites optimized
  - Conversion lift delivered
  - Case study results
  - Any other real traction metrics available
- Only use real numbers. Per brand rules: "Never make unsubstantiated claims."
**Design**: Metric cards or number bar. Similar to homepage metrics bar.

---

### S5: CTA
**Job**: Soft conversion. Route interested visitors to the product.
**Spec**:
- "See what Grona can do" link
**Key lines**:
- Headline: "See what Grona can do"
- Link: "Explore features" >> `/features`
- Secondary: "Read our latest updates" >> `/changelog`
**Design**: Simple. Not a hard sell. This page earned trust, now hand off to product pages.
